Oatmeal Cookies

Whisk together the oats, flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl. In another bowl, whisk together unsalte butter (melted) and egg. Stir in the honey. Add in the flour mixture and the raisins. Chill the cookie dough for 30 minutes. Drop the cookie dough into 15 rounded scoops onto the prepared sheet, and flatten slightly. Bake at 165°C for 11-14 minutes.

Fruity Muffins

Sift the flour and baking powder into a bowl. In another bowl, lightly beat the eggs, then stir in the melted butter, milk and honey. Add to the flour with the remaining ingredients. Combine quickly without overworking. Spoon the mixture into the muffin tin and bake for 20-25 minutes.
